How it Works

This calculates the amount due for two Houses who share a water meter but have a split water account. For an example enter under 'Unit B' as shown by the place holders and click on CALCULATE and then move onto 'Unit A'. Enter as shown by the place holders and click calculate. 'Unit A' is for the main house on the property, it also has the main water meter which feeds 'Unit B'.
